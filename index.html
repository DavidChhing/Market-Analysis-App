Bus Mall Research Program

How To Play

  1. Out of the 3 available options, select the image your most likely to buy
  2. Continue selecting images 15 times
  3. Once completed, hit the "See Totals" button to get a chart of your results.
  4. To play again, just click on another favorite image and continue doing so 15x. Your results will be added to the current chart
  5. Undernear the chart, you can change how you view the results by selecting the "chart type" dropdown and select one of the following
    • Line
    • Column
    • Bar
    • Pie
    • Doughnut
